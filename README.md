# 📊 Exchange Rate Impact on Imports in Pakistan

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on Pakistan's **Imports, GDP, and Exchange Rate** data from **2000–2024**.

The objective is to understand how economic growth and exchange rate fluctuations influence imports and provide actionable business insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze import trends in Pakistan
- Study GDP impact on imports
- Examine exchange rate behavior
- Generate visual insights from data
- Provide business recommendations

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

**Source:** World Bank

**Variables:**

- 📈 GDP (Billion USD)
- 📦 Imports (Billion USD)
- 💱 Exchange Rate (USD/PKR)

**Period:** 2000–2024

## 🔍 Key Findings

### 1. GDP Strongly Drives Imports

GDP and imports show a very strong positive relationship.

### 2. Exchange Rate Has Limited Impact

After controlling for GDP, the exchange rate is not statistically significant.

### 3. Economic Growth Increases Import Demand

As Pakistan's economy expands, demand for imported goods also rises.

---

## 💡 Business Insights

- Focus on economic growth policies.
- Encourage local production.
- Reduce dependency on imported goods.
